Preamble

We, the members of "Service to Mankind," registered under the Telangana Societies Act, 2001 with registration number 111 of 2023, come together with a shared vision to make a positive impact on society. Guided by a deep commitment to social welfare, justice, and humanitarian values, we hereby establish this constitution to govern our mission and actions. With an unwavering dedication to the betterment of humanity, we strive to empower the marginalized, uplift the downtrodden, and bring hope to the lives of those in need. Recognizing our responsibility towards creating positive change, we pledge to work relentlessly to improve education, health, environmental sustainability, and social well-being. Embracing the principles of compassion, equality, and community, we seek to create an environment of collaboration, mutual respect, and inclusivity. Our aim is to bridge gaps and dissolve barriers, regardless of caste, creed, gender, or socio-economic background. Drawing inspiration from the rich legacy of service to mankind, we hold ourselves accountable to the highest ethical standards and transparency. Our mission is to be a beacon of hope, a catalyst for positive transformation, and a force that drives lasting change in society. As witnesses to this shared commitment, we adopt and enact this constitution to serve as the bedrock of our journey, ensuring that our actions align with our vision and values.

Duly adopted on [Date of Adoption]
