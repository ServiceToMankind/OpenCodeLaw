Preamble

We, the members of the Service to Mankind, in order to form a more perfect organization, establish justice, insure domestic harmony, provide for the common good, promote the general welfare, and secure the blessings of liberty to ourselves and our posterity, do ordain and establish this Constitution for the Service to Mankind.
